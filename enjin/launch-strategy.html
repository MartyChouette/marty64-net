Recommendation: Lead with Wedge A, layer B and C.
  • Zero competition - no other engine targets the Flash community
  • Existing features - SWF import, AS2/AS3 transpiler, Newgrounds.io are already built
  • Viral potential - Flash nostalgia is a strong cultural current
  • HTML5 export is already working - shortest path to a proof game
  • Newgrounds is an active, engaged community with built-in discovery

Then layer in Retro-First (same audience overlap) and Accessibility-First (institutional credibility) as secondary messaging.

Key Growth Drivers

  • Post-Unity migration wave - pricing changes created lasting trust deficit
  • Flash game nostalgia + revival - no dedicated modern engine for the Flash community
  • Accessibility regulations - EU Accessibility Act 2025, US Section 508
  • Education market - built-in visual scripting, behavior trees, procgen for teaching
  • "Hit game" effect - a single commercially successful game boosts adoption dramatically

Mitigation Plan

RiskSeverityMitigation
Bus factor (single developer) Critical Write contributor docs. Modularize panels/systems. Define open-core boundary early.
Burnout Critical Stick to weekly/monthly cadence. No crunch. Ship small, ship often.
Godot momentum High Don't fight Godot on general purpose. Win on Flash revival, retro, accessibility.
Community stall High Micro jams, featured games, Discord engagement. Lower the bar for first success.
Feature creep Medium Freeze features for 90 days. Polish and ship what exists.
Console certification Low Focus PC + web. Partner with porting specialists later.
