How to Read Case Studies

Each case study: 1. Describes the specific design element 2. Analyzes how it implements concepts from this KB 3. Connects to broader principles 4. Questions what's unresolved or debatable Case studies aren't celebrations. They're examinations. Even great videogames have questionable choices.

Contributing Case Studies

If you want to add a case study: 1. Pick a specific element (not "Dark Souls is good" but "the bonfire as checkpoint") 2. Use concepts from this KB 3. Include your interpretation but also acknowledge other readings 4. Connect to at least 2-3 other entries
